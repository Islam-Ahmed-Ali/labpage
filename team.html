The core members driving research and innovation in intelligent energy systems and electrified mobility.

Lab Director

Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud

Associate Professor, Electrical & Computer Engineering
Kennesaw State University

Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud (Senior Member, IEEE) is an Associate Professor at Kennesaw State University. He received his Ph.D. from the University of Waterloo in 2016, specializing in photovoltaic systems, power electronics, protection, and real-time modeling.

He serves as Associate Editor for the IEEE Transactions on Energy Conversion and IEEE Power Engineering Letters. His work includes award-winning solar diagnostics, a sole-inventor U.S. patent, and recognition in Stanford's World’s Top 2% Scientists.

Ph.D. Researchers

Islam A. Sayed

Ph.D. Candidate, Electrical & Computer Engineering

Islam Sayed (Graduate Student Member, IEEE) earned his B.Sc. and M.Sc. from Cairo University and is currently pursuing his Ph.D. at Kennesaw State University with a Graduate Scholarship and Research Assistantship.

His research includes hybrid energy storage systems, reinforcement-learning-based EMS, KalmanNet SOC/SOH estimation, and real-time PIL/HIL validation. He was selected among the Top 20 Reviewers at IEEE ECCE 2025 and is a Full Member of Sigma Xi.
