Lab Director

Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud

Associate Professor, Electrical & Computer Engineering
Kennesaw State University

Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud (Senior Member, IEEE) is an Associate Professor at Kennesaw State University. He earned his Ph.D. from the University of Waterloo, Canada, in 2016. His research interests include solar photovoltaic systems, modeling, efficiency optimization, power electronics, control, protection, and fault diagnosis.

He serves as an Associate Editor for the IEEE Transactions on Energy Conversion and IEEE Power Engineering Letters. He has received several recognitions, including the Best Paper Award at the IEEE International Renewable Energy Congress and being listed among the World's Top 2% Scientists by Stanford University since 2020.

Ph.D. Researchers

Islam A. Sayed

Ph.D. Candidate, Electrical & Computer Engineering
Kennesaw State University

Islam A. Sayed (Graduate Student Member, IEEE) earned his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Electrical Engineering from Cairo University, Egypt, in 2017 and 2021. He is pursuing his Ph.D. at Kennesaw State University with a Graduate Scholarship and Research Assistantship.

His research interests include energy management systems (EMS), hybrid energy storage systems, battery/supercapacitor SOC and SOH estimation, reinforcement learning, and real-time validation using processor-in-the-loop and hardware-in-the-loop platforms.

He has authored multiple IEEE papers, serves as a reviewer for IEEE and Elsevier, was recognized among the Top 20 Reviewers at IEEE ECCE 2025, is a Full Member of Sigma Xi, and served as Session Co-Chair at IEEE IECON 2024.
