Latest Updates

Islam A. Sayed selected among Top 20 Reviewers at IEEE ECCE 2025

October 2025

Congratulations to Islam A. Sayed for being recognized among the Top 20 Reviewers in the Power Converters & Control Track at IEEE ECCE 2025 — one of the flagship conferences in power electronics. This recognition reflects outstanding technical contributions to the review process and professional service to the IEEE community.

IEEE Access publishes EV HESS + Solar Integration Review Paper

September 2025

The lab's comprehensive review titled "Energy Management of Battery–Supercapacitor Hybrid Storage in Electric Vehicles with Solar Integration" has been published in IEEE Access. The paper provides a unified overview of hybrid storage architectures, control strategies, and solar-assisted EV systems.

Three Papers Accepted at IEEE ECCE 2025

August 2025

The iENERGY Lab had three accepted papers at IEEE ECCE 2025, including oral presentations and a student demonstration. Topics include KalmanNet SOC estimation, SOH prediction, and real-time PIL implementation.

KalmanNet Supercapacitor SOC Estimator Published in Future Batteries

July 2025

The lab published a new article in Future Batteries (Elsevier) demonstrating the first KalmanNet-based SOC estimator for supercapacitor systems under nonlinear EV dynamics.

Islam A. Sayed Serves as Session Co-Chair at IEEE IECON 2024

December 2024

Islam served as Session Co-Chair in the Energy Storage and Power Electronics session at IEEE IECON 2024, contributing to technical leadership in the international power-electronics community.

New HIL Platform Added: Speedgoat Real-Time Target

October 2024

The lab expanded its real-time testing capabilities by integrating a Speedgoat Performance real-time system, enabling hardware-in-the-loop validation for energy management and control research.
