Lab News & Highlights

Major research milestones, peer-reviewed publications, conference presentations, and professional recognitions from the iENERGY Lab.

IEEE Access Review Paper Published

2025

The review article “Energy Management of Battery–Supercapacitor Hybrid Storage Systems in Electric Vehicles with Solar Integration”, authored by Islam A. Sayed and Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud, was published in IEEE Access, providing a comprehensive overview of hybrid energy storage systems, energy management strategies, and renewable energy integration for electric vehicles.

Future Batteries Journal Publication Released

2025

The paper “Robust Hybrid Neural–Kalman Filter for Real-Time Supercapacitor State-of-Charge Estimation in Electric Vehicles”, authored by Islam A. Sayed and Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud, was published in Future Batteries (Elsevier, Q1), presenting experimentally validated real-time state estimation results under dynamic EV operating conditions.

ECCE 2025 Student Project Demo Nomination

2025

Islam A. Sayed participated in the IEEE Student Project Demonstration Competition at IEEE ECCE 2025, where his KalmanNet-based Processor-in-the-Loop (PIL) demonstration for supercapacitor State-of-Charge estimation was nominated among the top demo projects under the leadership of IEEE Life Fellow Dr. Muhammad H. Rashid.

ECCE 2025 Oral & Poster Presentations

2025

At IEEE ECCE 2025, Islam A. Sayed presented two peer-reviewed papers: an oral presentation on battery State-of-Health estimation and a poster presentation on supercapacitor State-of-Charge estimation, co-authored with Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud.

Top 20 Reviewer Recognition – IEEE ECCE 2025

2025

Islam A. Sayed was recognized among the Top 20 Reviewers in the Power Converter Topologies & Control track at IEEE ECCE 2025, in recognition of his high-quality and timely peer-review contributions.

Sigma Xi Full Membership

2025

Islam A. Sayed was nominated and accepted as a Full Member of Sigma Xi, the Scientific Research Honor Society, recognizing his scholarly achievements and contributions to scientific research.

IECON 2024 Oral Presentations

2024

At IEEE IECON 2024, Islam A. Sayed delivered an oral presentation on battery State-of-Charge estimation for electric vehicles, while Mahmoud Houwaidi presented an oral paper on wireless power transfer systems for automated guided vehicles (AGVs), both co-authored with Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud.

KSU Fall 2024 Research Symposium

2024

Islam A. Sayed presented ongoing research on energy management systems and energy storage state estimation at the Kennesaw State University Fall 2024 Research Symposium.

Expansion of AGV Wireless Power Transfer Research

2024

Mahmoud Houwaidi, under the supervision of Dr. Yousef A. Mahmoud, established a dedicated research track on wireless power transfer for automated guided vehicles (AGVs), focusing on compensation networks, harmonic effects, coupling variation, and system-level optimization.

Energy Management & State Estimation Research Established

2024

The iENERGY Lab formally established its core research direction in energy management systems and state estimation for batteries and supercapacitors in electric vehicle applications.
