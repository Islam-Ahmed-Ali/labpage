Lab News & Highlights

Major publications, awards, conference presentations, and research milestones from the iENERGY Lab.

IEEE Access Review Paper Published

2025

The comprehensive review article “Energy Management of Battery–Supercapacitor Hybrid Storage Systems in Electric Vehicles with Solar Integration” was published in IEEE Access, summarizing recent advances in hybrid energy storage, energy management systems, and renewable energy integration for electric vehicles.

Future Batteries Journal Article Published

2025

The journal article “Robust Hybrid Neural–Kalman Filter for Real-Time Energy Storage State Estimation” was published in Future Batteries (Elsevier, Q1), presenting advanced approaches for reliable state estimation in battery and supercapacitor systems.

IEEE ECCE 2025 Project Demo Nomination

2025

The iENERGY Lab received a Project Demo Nomination at the IEEE Energy Conversion Congress & Expo (ECCE 2025) for a real-time energy storage system demonstration focused on intelligent state estimation and embedded implementation.

Sigma Xi Full Membership Nomination & Acceptance

2025

Islam A. Sayed was nominated and accepted as a Full Member of Sigma Xi, The Scientific Research Honor Society, in recognition of research contributions and scholarly activity.

Top 20 Reviewer Recognition – IEEE ECCE 2025

2025

Islam A. Sayed was recognized among the Top 20 Reviewers in the Power Converter Topologies & Control track at IEEE ECCE 2025 for outstanding peer-review contributions.

IEEE ECCE 2025 Oral & Poster Presentations

2025

The iENERGY Lab presented two papers at IEEE ECCE 2025, including an oral presentation on battery State-of-Health estimation and a poster presentation on supercapacitor State-of-Charge estimation for electric vehicle applications.

IECON 2024 Oral Presentations on Battery and Wireless Power Transfer

October 2024

At the 50th Annual Conference of the IEEE Industrial Electronics Society (IECON 2024), the lab delivered oral presentations on battery State-of-Charge estimation and wireless power transfer for automated guided vehicles (AGVs).

KSU Fall 2024 Research Symposium Presentation

Fall 2024

Research from the iENERGY Lab was presented at the Kennesaw State University Fall 2024 Research Symposium, highlighting advances in energy management and electrified mobility systems.

Energy Management and State Estimation Research Established

2024

The iENERGY Lab formally established a dedicated research track in energy management systems and state estimation for batteries and supercapacitors in electric vehicle and hybrid energy storage applications.

Expansion of AGV Wireless Power Transfer Research

2024

The lab expanded its research activities in wireless power transfer (WPT) for automated guided vehicles (AGVs), focusing on inductance variation, harmonic effects, compensation networks, and system robustness under misalignment conditions.
