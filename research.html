Research Areas

The iENERGY Lab conducts research in intelligent energy systems and electrified mobility, with an emphasis on electric vehicles, hybrid energy storage, wireless power transfer, and renewable energy integration. Our work spans modeling, control, optimization, and real-time implementation of advanced power and energy systems.
