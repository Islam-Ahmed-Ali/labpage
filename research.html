Research Areas

Advancing Intelligent Energy Systems for Electrified Mobility

Overview

The iENERGY Lab conducts research at the intersection of artificial intelligence, power electronics, electrochemical energy storage, real-time embedded systems, and advanced control. Our research aims to develop intelligent, efficient, and robust energy solutions for next-generation electric vehicles (EVs), distributed energy systems, and sustainable transportation infrastructures.
