iENERGY Lab

Intelligent Energy Systems & Electrified Mobility Laboratory

The iENERGY Lab focuses on energy management, state estimation, hybrid energy storage, wireless power transfer, and AI-based control for electric vehicles and renewable energy systems.

Explore Research

Research Highlights

Energy Management Systems

Design and analysis of energy management strategies for electric vehicles using battery–supercapacitor hybrid storage systems.

State Estimation

State-of-Charge (SOC) and State-of-Health (SOH) estimation for batteries and supercapacitors under dynamic operating conditions.

Wireless Power Transfer

High-efficiency wireless charging systems for electric vehicles and automated guided vehicles, including compensation networks and harmonic analysis.

Machine & Deep Learning Control

Application of machine learning and deep learning techniques to power electronics, control systems, and energy optimization.

Hybrid Energy Storage Systems

Integration of batteries, supercapacitors, and renewable sources for improved performance and reliability in electrified mobility.

Renewable Energy Integration

Integration of solar photovoltaic systems with electric vehicles and energy storage platforms.
