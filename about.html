About the iENERGY Lab

The iENERGY Lab – Intelligent Energy Systems & Electrified Mobility Laboratory at Kennesaw State University conducts advanced research in state estimation for batteries and supercapacitors, energy management systems for electric vehicles, hybrid energy storage systems, renewable energy integration—particularly solar photovoltaic (PV) systems, wireless power transfer, and machine learning– and deep learning–based control of power electronic systems.

Our work integrates physics-based modeling, machine learning, real-time embedded control, experimental validation, and system-level optimization to develop reliable, efficient, and intelligent electrified mobility and renewable energy technologies.
